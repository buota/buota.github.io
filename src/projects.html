Fleeced


Fantasy Sports Mobile App

Fleeced is a fantasy sports forum where users can connect with other passionate fantasy enthusiasts! Our purpose is to provide a dedicated place for fantasy sports lovers to share and connect with others.

Features

  • Post polls for start/sit and trade decisions
  • Vote on other users' polls to help them decide
  • Connect with a community of fantasy sports fans
  • Rank up by making the correct predictions!
