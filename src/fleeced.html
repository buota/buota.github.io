*** Fleeced - Fantasy Sports Mobile App ***

Fleeced


Fantasy Sports Mobile App

Fleeced is a fantasy sports forum where users can discuss trades, debate start/sit decisions, and engage with a community of fellow managers.

My Role

I coded this entire project — frontend, backend, and all app logic — using Kotlin. I collaborated with a teammate on the database design and setup as he is building our web app! Beyond development, I led customer development efforts, conducting user interviews and surveys to validate market demand. Our research indicated strong user interest, with a percentage of respondents expressing willingness to pay for enhanced insights and analytics.

Features

  • Post polls for start/sit and trade decisions
  • Vote on other users' polls to help them decide
  • Weekly, seasonal, and all-time leaderboard
  • Rank up by making the correct predictions!
